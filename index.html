Frequently asked questions

Should I exercise every day?

No, you dont have to exercise every day. Adding 1-2 rest days per week is beneficial for muscle recovery and helps prevent injury.

How long should my workout be?

The length of your workout should match your fitness goals and current fitness level.

What’s the most effective way to lose fat?

Combining strength training with aerobic exercises is a proven method for fat loss.

How can I build lean muscle?

To build lean muscle, focus on heavy strength training, ensure adequate protein intake, and prioritize sufficient rest.

Will lifting weights make me bulky?

Lifting weights alone won’t make you bulky. Significant muscle growth requires targeted training and nutrition strategies.
